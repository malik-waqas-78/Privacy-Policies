Privacy Policy for Momentum

Last updated: August 31, 2025

Momentum is a private, offline-first habit tracker designed to help you build and maintain positive habits without distractions. We are committed to protecting your privacy, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection and Storage

Momentum is designed to work entirely offline. All your habit data, including habit names, streaks, and completion records, is stored securely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers or third-party services. Your data remains solely on your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Permissions Used

  • No Internet Access Required: Momentum does not use your internet connection. The app functions fully offline without syncing your data to the cloud.
  • android.permission.VIBRATE: This permission is used only to provide optional haptic feedback (vibrations) when checking off a habit. This feature can be disabled in the app settings.

No Ads or Tracking

Momentum does not display advertisements, use analytics, or track your behavior. We do not use advertising identifiers or any third-party tracking services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
