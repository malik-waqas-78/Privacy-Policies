Privacy Policy for Expense Tracker

Last updated: August 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the Service and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Expense Tracker is a completely local application. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All information you enter into the app—including your expenses, categories, and budgets—is stored only on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or similar identifiers.
  • Financial Information: We do not collect bank account numbers, card numbers, or credentials.
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you use the app.
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, IP address, or other device metadata.

On-Device Storage & Data Control

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete your data at any time by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app. We do not have access to your data.

Permissions

  • android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Used to send local notifications and reminders related to your expenses and budgets. Notifications are generated and managed locally on your device and are not sent to external servers.

How We Configure SDKs

  • We only include SDKs that are necessary for app functionality.
  • Where supported, we disable personalized data collection by default unless strictly needed (e.g., for crash diagnosis).
  • If an SDK is optional (e.g., Analytics/Ads), we will show an in-app notice or setting before enabling it (if implemented in your build).

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data to us, please contact us and we will assist with deletion instructions (local data resides only on the device).

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new Privacy Policy on this page and update the “Last updated” date above. Changes are effective when posted.
