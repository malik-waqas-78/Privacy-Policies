Privacy Policy for AuraFlow

Last updated: September 1, 2025

AuraFlow is a simple, beautiful, and offline-first mindful breathing guide. It helps users relax and focus through guided, animated breathing exercises with customizable session parameters. This Privacy Policy describes how AuraFlow collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Information Collection and Use

AuraFlow is designed to be an offline application. We do not collect any personal data from our users. All data, including your breathing session history and app settings, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Permissions

AuraFlow requests the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE (android.permission.VIBRATE): This permission is used to provide optional haptic feedback to guide breathing cycles, enhancing the user experience.
  • INTERNET (android.permission.INTERNET): This permission is included for potential future integration of advertisements or analytics services. Currently, AuraFlow does not utilize this permission for data transmission.
  • AD_ID (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID): This permission is included to support potential future monetization through advertising. Currently, AuraFlow does not display ads or use this permission.

Local Data Storage

AuraFlow uses Jetpack DataStore for storing user preferences (e.g., theme choice, default duration, haptic feedback preference) and Room Database for storing breathing session history. This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not accessible by us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
