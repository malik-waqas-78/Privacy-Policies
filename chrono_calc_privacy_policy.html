Privacy Policy for ChronoCalc

Last updated: September 1, 2025

ChronoCalc is an advanced scientific calculator that integrates a calculation history directly into the main interface. It helps users perform complex calculations and easily reference past entries. This Privacy Policy describes how ChronoCalc collects, uses, and discloses your information.

Information Collection and Use

ChronoCalc is designed to be an offline application. We do not collect any personal data from our users. All data, including your calculation history and app settings, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Permissions

ChronoCalc requests the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE (android.permission.VIBRATE): This permission is used to provide optional haptic feedback on key presses, enhancing the user experience.
  • INTERNET (android.permission.INTERNET): This permission is included for potential future integration of advertisements or analytics services. Currently, ChronoCalc does not utilize this permission for data transmission.
  • AD_ID (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID): This permission is included to support potential future monetization through advertising. Currently, ChronoCalc does not display ads or use this permission.

Local Data Storage

ChronoCalc uses Jetpack DataStore for storing user preferences (e.g., theme choice, haptic feedback preference) and Room Database for storing calculation history. This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not accessible by us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
