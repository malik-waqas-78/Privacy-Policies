Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Apotekare Exam Bank handles personal information and study progress when you use the mobile app.

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Apotekare Exam Bank is an exam preparation app. It is not a medical service, does not provide medical advice, and should not be used for diagnosis, treatment, or emergency care.

Information We Collect

When you use the app, we may collect and store:

  • Your email address and account identifier for login.
  • Your display name if you provide one during registration.
  • Your quiz answers, progress strings, topic progress, and session history.
  • Basic technical information needed to keep the app secure and functional.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Authenticate your account.
  • Save and sync your exam progress across sessions and devices.
  • Show quiz history, scores, and completion status.
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the app.

Firebase and Cloud Services

The app uses Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Firebase may process information according to Google Firebase terms and privacy practices. You can learn more at Firebase Privacy and Security .

Offline Storage

The app stores exam topics, questions, progress, and pending sync operations locally on your device so the quiz experience can continue when internet access is unavailable. Pending changes are synced when connectivity becomes available.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with advertisers. Information may be processed by service providers that are necessary to operate the app, such as Firebase.

Data Retention

We keep account and progress data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the app. Local data may remain on your device until you delete the app, clear app storage, or sign out if the app is configured to clear local data on sign-out.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Use the password reset flow to recover access to your account.
  • Reset topic progress inside the app.
  • Request deletion of account-related data by contacting the app owner.
  • Delete the app from your device to remove locally stored app data.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for students and professionals preparing for exams. It is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact the app owner so the data can be reviewed and removed when appropriate.

Security

We use Firebase security controls and app-side access checks to help protect account and progress data. No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so you should use a strong password and keep your device protected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact
