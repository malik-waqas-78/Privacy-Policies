Echo Match Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 6, 2025

Data Storage and Collection

Echo Match is designed with your privacy in mind. The app:

  • Stores all game data (scores, settings) locally on your device
  • Does not collect or transmit any personal information
  • Does not use analytics or tracking
  • Does not require an internet connection

Permissions

Echo Match requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Only used to open external links (privacy policy, GitHub repository)
  • VIBRATE: Used for haptic feedback during gameplay

Data Deletion

All app data can be removed by either:

  • Using the "Clear Scores" option in the High Scores screen
  • Clearing app data through your device's Settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Echo Match is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted here and reflected in the app's "About" screen.
