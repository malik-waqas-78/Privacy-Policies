Privacy Policy for Quick Notes App

Last updated: August 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Quick Notes App ("the App") operates with respect to your privacy.

1. Data Collection and Use

The Quick Notes App is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information from your device. All notes and data you create within the App are stored locally on your device and are not accessible by us or any third parties.

  • No Personal Data Collected: We do not collect your name, email address, location, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Data Collected: We do not collect information about how you use the App, such as features accessed, frequency of use, or crash reports.
  • Local Storage Only: All your notes and associated data are stored exclusively on your device's local storage. We do not have access to this data.

2. Permissions

The Quick Notes App requires no special permissions to function. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive data on your device. It only uses internet permission to display this privacy policy.

3. Third-Party Services

The Quick Notes App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or other services that might collect your data.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
