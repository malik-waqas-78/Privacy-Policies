Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 4, 2026

Welcome to Paws & Patches. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a memorable experience for your furry friends, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address for authentication and profile management.
  • Pet Profiles: We store the information you provide about your pets, including names, biographies, traits, birth dates, and location (City/Country).
  • Pet Photos: Images you upload of your pets are stored securely to display in their scrapbook and sharing features.
  • Moments & Journaling: Text and photos added to your pet's "moments" timeline.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Paws & Patches service.
  • Personalize your experience and remember your pet's special dates.
  • Allow you to share your pet's profile with the "Explore" community if you choose.
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback.
"Your pet's memories are yours. We do not sell your personal data to third parties."

3. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard cloud infrastructure (Firebase) to store your data and photos securely. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Sharing with the Community

The "Explore" section of the app allows other users to see registered pets. By listing a pet, its name, bio, traits, and photo may be visible to other members of the Paws & Patches pack.

5. Your Choices & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can edit or delete pet profiles and associated moments at any time within the app. Deleting a pet profile successfully removes all associated data and images from our active databases.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
