Privacy Policy for FlagFlare

Last Updated: 2025-09-07

1. Introduction

Welcome to FlagFlare ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application, FlagFlare (the "App").

Our App is designed to be a completely offline experience. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any of the following information:

  • Personal Information: We do not ask for or collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect any data about how you use the App, such as your quiz scores, progress, or preferences. All such data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us.
  • Device Information: We do not collect any information about your device, such as its model, operating system, or unique identifiers.

3. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE: This permission is used to provide haptic feedback during gameplay. No data is collected or transmitted.
  • INTERNET: This permission is included solely to allow you to open this Privacy Policy and other external links from within the app. The app itself does not make any network requests to our servers.

4. Data Storage

All data related to your use of the App, such as your quiz history and settings, is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the App, this data will be permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
